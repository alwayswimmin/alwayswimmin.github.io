Motivation

Victoria asked me what I think about when I'm on the road, not working on anything. I didn't have much to say; I could only recall pondering embarrassing things I've said or done in the past. I then recalled Peter Thiel's famous interview question: "Tell me something that's true, that almost nobody agrees with you on." I realized I didn't have much I could say for this, either. All of my beliefs are fairly mainstream, which was disappointing for someone who claims to be reasonably well-read in philosophy and economics. I aim, through blogging, defending, rigorizing, and amending my beliefs, to be able to draw conclusions that are original in their own right. This website is the first of hopefully many personal projects to develop my originality and thought.
